## 📋 Overview

An interactive **Customer Marketing Analytics Dashboard** built with Streamlit that helps marketing teams analyze customer demographics, purchase behavior, and campaign response patterns. Perfect for understanding customer segments and optimizing marketing strategies.

### Key Features

- ✅ **Customer Demographics Analysis** - Age, income, education, and marital status distribution
- ✅ **Purchase Behavior Tracking** - Spending patterns across 6 product categories (Wines, Meat, Fish, etc.)
- ✅ **Campaign Response Analytics** - Identify which customers respond to marketing campaigns
- ✅ **Channel Performance** - Compare Web vs Catalog vs Store purchases
- ✅ **Interactive Filters** - Filter by education, marital status, country, and income range
- ✅ **Correlation Analysis** - Understand relationships between customer attributes
- ✅ **Data Export** - Download filtered datasets for further analysis

## 📊 Sample Insights You Can Discover

- Which education level has the highest average income?
- Do married customers spend more than single customers?
